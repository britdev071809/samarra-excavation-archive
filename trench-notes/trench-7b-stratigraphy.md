# Stratigraphic Report: Trench 7B

## Location
Grid Square: N12-E45

## Excavation Dates
- Start: 2023-05-15
- End: 2023-06-20

## Stratigraphic Units

### SU 101
- Depth: 0.0–0.5 m
- Description: Topsoil, mixed modern deposits.

### SU 102
- Depth: 0.5–1.2 m
- Description: Dark brown clay with scattered pottery sherds.
- Dating: Late Abbasid (9th–10th century CE).

### SU 103
- Depth: 1.2–1.8 m
- Description: Compact sandy layer with ash lenses.
- **Notable find**: Fragments of mould‑blown glass vessels (Type G‑7b).
- Dating: **Early Abbasid (late 8th century CE)** – based on associated pottery and coin.

### SU 104
- Depth: 1.8–2.5 m
- Description: Sterile riverine silt.

## Interpretation
The glassware from SU 103 represents an early Abbasid occupation phase, possibly linked to the initial construction of the palace quarter.
